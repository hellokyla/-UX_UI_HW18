highlighted projects

FTC Website Redesign

This project was designed to rethink and redesign a federal agencies website. A goal of this project was to better highlight the useful resources the FTC had to offer for consumers and businesses.

  • Based on 16 user interviews and multiple surveys this site went through 8 different design iterations to arrive at the final high fidelity prototype.

  • This was achieved by creating a tabbed structure that allows the user to navigate and filter the site based on their background (government, consumer, or business).

  • Tools: Adobe XD and Affinity Designer

Group Travel App - TGTHR

This app was developed as a new and unique travel mobile application. After interviews and industry research the niche chosen was to address the struggle of planning travel with a group.

  • Competitor analysis was crucial in this development; identified helpful features and avoided filler content.

  • A creative solution within this app is the anonymous polling feature developed from user insights. Users can receive honest feedback without their companions editing their answers to appease social pressure.

  • Tools: Adobe XD and Miro
